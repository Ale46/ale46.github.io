What is CompaFood?

CompaFood is an app that allow you to get info about foods. You can easily have an idea of what you're eating, plus you can compare foods stats to better balance your diet.

How can I search a food?

The Action Button is the first step, always.. After hitting the search button you have to just type what you're looking for. There's the possibility to also scan the barcode of a food to guess what you want to search (BETA).

Food nutritions data

You can easily get nutritions info of the searched food and add the food to the comparison list.

Comparison list

In order to compare food you need to first search and add two food. After that you're ready to get your comparison
