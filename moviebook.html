Home of the MovieBook project, showing the currently most watched movies


Screen with the most important info of a movie with the possibilities to add to different types of lists


You can easily remove a movie from a list


You have your personal movies list page where you can track what you watched or want to watch


MovieBook want to be a pseudo social network where you can share, track and discover movies. After you join it, you will be able to track what you watched, you want to watch but also know what your friends do. Another important feature is to know the most important things about a movie like the overview, the cast or even the trailer.

All is working with the Facebook friends list, infact there isn't an option to add friend. If someone is already your friend on Facebook you will see all his activities. With the integrated rating system you can express your judgments but also discover new good movies based on friends votes.
